By using this blog I aim to make the journey through machine learning concepts easy by using illustrations, code and mathematical explanations.

**About Me**  
I am currently a Data Scientist working at [PromoFarma](https://www.promofarma.es/) in Barcelona, Spain. Alumni of the Master Degree in Data Science at [Universitat Oberta de Catalunya](https://estudios.uoc.edu/es/masters-universitarios/data-science/presentacion).

Apart from my Master's Degree, I received education from top institutions using massive online courses like the [DeepLearning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/account/accomplishments/specialization/T8KNP72GKT38) or the [Machine Learning Certification](https://www.coursera.org/account/accomplishments/verify/8HZNJSANJUF2) by Stanford University. I have a passion for learning new things, so feel free to recommend me books, courses or technologies to check out.

I initially began my career as a data analyst but transitioned to data science through rigorous practice. Some of the projects I worked on during this process can be found [here](https://github.com/josumsc/).
